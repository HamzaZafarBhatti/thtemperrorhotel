Multiwall Industrial Paper Bags

Hasrat Meranti Sdn Bhd (219214-D)

Hasrat Meranti Sdn Bhd ("HM") commenced operations in 1991. Today, HM is a globally recognised regional leader in the provision of multiwall paper sack packaging solutions.

We introduce smarter packaging solutions and to assist customers to improve the packaging performance with high-performance quality products and in cost-effective way. Our introduction of such innovations to the market will provide cement customers with efficient dust free filling bags without the need for perforations. Customers who tap into such innovations will benefit in their cost control and operations in the long run.

Our main manufacturing facilities are in Kanthan and Tapah, Perak.

Equipped with advance machinery, technically competent and experienced workforce, an effective ISO 9001:2008 Quality Management System, HM's products are sold to the local market and exported and our customers are from industries such as cement, building materials, calcium carbonate, chemical, food, hydrated lime and a host of others that use paper bags as it packaging medium.
